This page explains how to request deletion of your account in the Cardsto app, operated by DrashVig Techno Services Private Limited.

How to Delete Your Account

To delete your account, please send an email to:

📧 techdrashvig@gmail.com

Subject: Account Deletion Request

Include your registered email address or mobile number

What Happens After Deletion

Once your request is processed:

  • Your account will be permanently deleted
  • Your profile and business card data will be removed

Processing Time

Your request will be completed within 5–7 working days.
